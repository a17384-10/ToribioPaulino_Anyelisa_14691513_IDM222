La Llorona Hair Care!

This project was the first one I felt very proud of, even if it is only a poster with a concept tied to it.

This project was made for a class I had taken not too long ago, and within it, I made a mindmap of different facts about La Llorona and how I could include her folklore within the product I was to create while also keeping in mind that her folklore is a grand part of Mexcian culture, and making sure that we are aware of such.

Take your time to read through the mindmap and the connection to why I chose to make a hair-care product for her!
